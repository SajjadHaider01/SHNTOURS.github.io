Welcome To GIBCE

The Government Institute of Business and Commercial Education (GIBCE) Malir is the one of best institutions in the Province of Sindh running under the administrative control of Sindh Technical Education and Vocational Training Authority (STEVTA).

It is offering mainly two years Diploma in Commerce in Accounting, Finance, Banking, Insurance and Secretarial Practices which is equivalent to Inter (Commerce) besides other courses of Ecommerce, Chinese Language, DIT, CIT, Media Developer and Mobile Soft/hardware courses.

It was established in the year of 1973 primarily offered short course in English Typewriting and English Shorthand. Among others, it was upgraded and renamed as Government College of Education in Commercial Practices in the year of 2002 and thereafter as GIBCE in the year of 2010.

So far thousands of students have graduated from this prestigious institute and have successfully got jobs both nationally and internationally supporting their families and the country as well in foreign remittances.

The graduates of GIBCEs have further numerous opportunities for acquiring higher qualifications as well. There is a qualified and experienced faculty (Male & Female) in every discipline f course under the management and supervision of a Principal having huge experience in managerial capacities and the cooperative staff. There is also State-of-Art Airconditioned Computer Labs connected with speedy internet connection with latest versions of Computer series, Media Lab fully equipped with brand new and latest equipment, Cameras, Library with clean, tidy and spacious Classrooms, Play Area for Indoor Games, Standby Generator, round the clock Surveillance System and measures are ensured for strict Security of students both (Male and Female), staff and visitors, spacious and secure parking area, Green Lawn, clean separate Washrooms for Male and Female. Regular Classes, individual attention on the students, Career Counseling and also job placement advises.
